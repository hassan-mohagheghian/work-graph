# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation

### Objectives

Establish the core collaboration platform.

### Deliverables

* Authentication
* Organizations
* Memberships
* Projects
* Tasks
* Role management

Outcome:

Teams can manage projects and tasks.

---

## Phase 2 — Knowledge Layer

### Objectives

Create a shared organizational memory.

### Deliverables

* Documents
* Document editing
* Document search
* Knowledge organization

Outcome:

Teams can preserve and manage knowledge.

---

## Phase 3 — AI Planning

### Objectives

Transform knowledge into execution plans.

### Deliverables

* Goal extraction
* Roadmap generation
* Milestone generation
* Task generation
* Document summarization

Outcome:

Teams can generate plans directly from requirements and documentation.

---

## Phase 4 — AI Knowledge

### Objectives

Enable AI-powered organizational understanding.

### Deliverables

* Embeddings
* Semantic search
* RAG
* Organizational assistant

Outcome:

Users can interact with organizational knowledge through AI.

---

## Phase 5 — Goal Alignment

### Objectives

Ensure execution remains aligned with strategy.

### Deliverables

* Progress analysis
* Goal alignment scoring
* Risk detection
* Recommendation engine

Outcome:

WorkGraph acts as a project navigation system.

---

## Phase 6 — Observability Intelligence

### Objectives

Connect operational signals with organizational goals.

### Deliverables

* Telemetry collection
* Log analysis
* Metrics integration
* Deployment insights

Outcome:

WorkGraph understands both planning and execution.

---

## Phase 7 — Platform Evolution

### Objectives

Scale architecture and capabilities.

### Deliverables

* Service extraction
* Advanced analytics
* Enterprise integrations

Outcome:

WorkGraph becomes an enterprise-grade operating system.
